About Karpool

As three students that go to school at UCSD, we thought of reasons why we have not gone out to explore the world around us aka San Diego. One of the reasons we came up with was that we did not have the methods of transportation necessary to go exploring (this was before Uber/Lyft/etc.). As a student without a car, we would have to bus, walk, bike, or hitch rides from other friends that had cars to get to the places we wanted to go. Seeing as how we all struggled with this, we figured that other college students could potentially also have this same problem. Thus Karpool was created. It is a way for people to be able to explore the world around them by going with others that are also exploring the world around them. You can make new friends, go places you have never gone before, and create new memories that will last a lifetime. Plus it's not as expensive as other ride sharing apps if you are the passenger, and you can save on gas money if you are the driver.
